Being a student is often shown in the media as a three-year non-stop party. In reality, many students find adapting to student life difficult. Reminded hopes to highlight this distorted reality in order to help students realise that they are not alone in the difficulties they are facing and to destigmatise mental health among students. Here are some strategies for coping with stressful situations:

Student Mental Health

Unfair Comparisons

Don't make unfair comparisons with other people - just because it might look like someone's having a better time than you doesn't mean it's true - social media doesn't show the party animal in bed watching netflix.

Catastophizing

Don't catatrophize - you can't predict the future and you're more resilient than you think and imagining the worst case senario isn't constructive - remember that you can do anything you put your mind to.
