Being a student is often shown in the media as a three-year non-stop party. In reality, many students find adapting to student life difficult. Reminded hopes to highlight this distorted reality in order to help students realise that they are not alone in the difficulties they are facing and to destigmatise mental health among students. Strategies

  • Don't make unfair comparisons to other people - just because it might look like someones having a better time than you doesn't mean its true - social media doesn't show the party animal in bed watching netflix
  • Don't catatrophize - you can't predict the future and you're more resilient than you think and imagining the worst case senario isn't constructive - remember that you can do anything you put your mind to
  • Reward yourself - experiencing stress is a natural part of life make sure you remember to reward yourself for the small victories of the day - Rome wasn't built in a day
